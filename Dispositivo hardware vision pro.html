General

The Apple Vision Pro is a revolutionary mixed-reality headset that offers a three-dimensional interface controlled with eyes, hands, and voice.

Características

  • Advanced three-dimensional camera
  • Eye and face tracking technology
  • High-resolution display offering more pixels than a 4K TV for each eye

Beneficios

With its spatial computing capabilities, the Apple Vision Pro enhances productivity and creativity, offering an infinite canvas for apps and a more immersive experience.
